# **App Name**: EthioMart

## Core Features:

- Role-Based User Authentication: Secure registration and login functionality supporting distinct roles for customers, sellers, and administrators, with role-based access control.
- Product Discovery & Search: Customers can intuitively browse products by category, view detailed product information, and perform full-text searches across product names and descriptions.
- Shopping Cart & Secure Checkout: Customers can add and manage items in a dynamic shopping cart, proceeding through a secure, multi-step checkout with multiple Ethiopian payment options (Telebirr, CBE Birr, Cash on Delivery).
- Seller Product & Order Management: Approved sellers have a dedicated portal to efficiently list new products, update existing inventory, and manage customer orders pertaining to their specific store.
- Admin Dashboard for Platform Oversight: Administrators gain a centralized dashboard to oversee and manage user accounts, approve or reject seller applications, moderate product listings, and access platform-wide analytics.
- Personalized Product Recommendations: An AI tool provides tailored product suggestions to customers based on their browsing history, past purchase patterns, and similarity to products they've viewed or added to their wishlist.
- Order Tracking & Wishlist Management: Customers can conveniently track the real-time status of their orders with a visual timeline and curate a personal wishlist of desired products.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant Ethiopian orange-red (#E85D2F), embodying the rich culture and energetic market of Ethiopia.
- Accent color: A rich saffron yellow (#F5C518), providing visual highlights and complementing the primary hue.
- Background color: A soft, creamy off-white (#FDF6E3), offering a clean and warm canvas for content.
- Secondary earth tone: A grounding deep brown (#8B4513), used for elements requiring a natural, warm anchor.
- Heading font: 'Outfit' (sans-serif), for a modern and bold display in titles and prominent text. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body font: 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (sans-serif), ensuring excellent readability for all main content. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Utilize 'lucide-react' icons to maintain a consistent, modern, and minimalist visual language across the platform.
- Fully responsive design, incorporating mobile-first principles with features like collapsible sidebars and bottom sheets for an optimal experience on any device.
- Interactive cards with rounded corners (rounded-2xl) and subtle shadow transitions (shadow-sm on hover to shadow-lg) for product displays and interactive elements.
- Implement content loading skeletons (shadcn Skeleton) on all data-intensive views to enhance perceived performance and user experience.
- Empty states will feature illustrative graphics and clear call-to-action buttons, guiding users to relevant functionalities.
- Engage users with smooth and meaningful page transitions, as well as subtle interactive hover states, powered by Framer Motion.
